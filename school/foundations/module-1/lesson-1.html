Lesson 1: Numbers Are Stories, Not Symbols

Goal: Understand that numbers represent amounts and meaning, not just digits.

Hook

If I write 1,000, do you feel how big that is? Most people don’t. Today we fix that.

The Hack

A number is a story about size:

  • Small numbers = close, personal
  • Big numbers = farther away
  • Digits matter after you understand the scale

Think of numbers like distance, not ink on paper.

Standard Method

Numbers represent quantities. Larger numbers represent greater amounts. Place value helps describe size.

Guided Practice

  1. Which is larger: 900 or 1,200? Explain without subtracting.
  2. Which feels bigger: 50 or 500? What changed?
  3. Circle the number that represents “a lot”: 8, 80, 800. Explain in one sentence.

Boss Problem

A school has 1,050 students. Another has 950. Without subtracting, explain which is bigger and how you know.

Exit Check

True or False: Numbers only matter because of their digits.

Answer

False. Digits matter after you understand size/scale.
