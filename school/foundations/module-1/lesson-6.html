Lesson 6: Estimation as a Superpower

Goal: Estimate to check answers and solve faster.

Hook

Estimation is how you catch wrong answers instantly. It’s the “smell test” for math.

The Hack

Round → Compute → Adjust (if needed)

  • Round numbers to friendly landmarks.
  • Do quick math.
  • Use it to check if the exact answer makes sense.

Standard Method

Estimation uses rounded values to find an approximate answer. It helps verify reasonableness.

Guided Practice

  1. Estimate: 398 + 602
  2. Estimate: 4,901 − 2,098
  3. Estimate: 49 × 21
  4. A calculator says 1,999 + 502 = 2,401. Does that seem right? Why?

Boss Problem

You’re buying snacks. Chips cost $2.49, a drink costs $1.89, and candy costs $0.99. Estimate the total without exact adding. Then explain whether $4.00 is enough.

Exit Check

Estimate: 7,950 + 1,020

Answer

~9,000 (8,000 + 1,000)
